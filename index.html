Hi, welcome to the site. Here's an overview.


The JROTC mission is simply, “ To Motivate Young People To Be Better Citizens ”. The JROTC Curriculum was designed to support the program objectives, which are to develop in each student (Cadet): Good Citizenship, self-reliance, leadership, responsiveness to authority, knowledge of military skills, ability to communicate effectively both orally and in writing, and an appreciation of the importance of physical education in the accomplishment of these objectives. The program is an excellent vehicle for demonstrating practical application of leadership and citizenship taught in the other high school courses. There are many extra-curricular activities in which your son/daughter may participate. These include the Marksmanship, Drill, Colorguard, Rifle, Exhibition, Raider Team (Fitness Team), Orienteering Teams, Academic and Tug O War. All of these activities provide an excellent means of teaching obedience to orders, correct posture and teamwork. These teams will compete with similar teams from other JROTC Schools in Florida. We also travel to other states to compete and ask your support when we do travel outside the local area.


Please note, The Junior Reserve Officer’s Training Corps (JROTC) Program at Boca Ciega High School is not designed to prepare your child for military service, nor will the training received hasten his/her being called upon to serve in any branch of the Armed Forces.
